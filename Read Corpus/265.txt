thumb america national game is book by albert spalding published in that details the early history of the sport of baseball much of the story is told first hand since since the spalding had been involved in the game first as player and later as an administrator in addition to his personal recollections he had access to the records of henry chadwick the game first statistician and archivist spalding was however said to aggrandise his role in the major moments in baseball history see also history of baseball references
