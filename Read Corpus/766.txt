crandall university is small christian liberal arts university located in moncton new brunswick canada crandall is operated by the canadian baptists of atlantic canada history the school was founded in under the name united baptist bible training school ubbts and served as both secondary school and bible school over two decades the focus of the school gradually shifted toward post secondary programs in ubbts became bible and junior christian liberal arts college and in the name was changed to atlantic baptist college abc sustained campaign to expand the school faculty and improve the level of education resulted in abc being able to grant full bachelor of arts degrees in its campus at this time was located along the salisbury road west of moncton central business district the institution moved to new campus built on the gorge road north of the central business district in the name was changed to atlantic baptist university abu reflection of expanded student enrollment and academic accreditation in the abu sports teams adopted the name the blue tide the institution was the first and thus far only english language university in moncton the atlantic baptist university act was passed by the legislative assembly of new brunswick in on august it was announced that the institution had changed its name to crandall university in honour of rev joseph crandall pioneering baptist minister in the maritime region in conjunction with the university name change crandall athletics took on new identity as the crandall chargers controversy in crandall university came under
