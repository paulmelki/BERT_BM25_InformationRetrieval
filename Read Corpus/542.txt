alger of liège known also as alger of cluny and algerus magister was learned clergyman from liège author of several notable works alger was first deacon of church of st bartholomew in his native liège and was then appointed to st lambert cathedral he declined offers from german bishops and finally retired to the monastery of cluny where he died at high age leaving behind solid reputation for piety and intelligence his history of the church of liège and many of his other works are lost the most important remaining are de misericordia et justitia on mercy and justice collection of biblical extracts and sayings of church fathers with commentary an important work for the history of church law and discipline which is to be found in the anecdota of martène vol de sacramentis corporis et sanguinis domini treatise in three books against the berengarian heresy highly commended by peter of cluny and erasmus de gratia et libero arbitrio given in bernard pez anecdota vol iv de sacrificio missae given in the collectio scriptor vet of angelo mai vol ix references
