triumphus caesari by andreani after painting by mantegna andrea andreani was an italian engraver on wood who was among the first printmakers in italy to use chiaroscuro which required multiple colours born and generally active in mantua about brulliot says and died at rome in his engravings are scarce and valuable and are chiefly copies of mantegna albrecht dürer parmigianino and titian the most remarkable of his works are mercury and ignorance the deluge pharaoh host drowned in the red sea after titian the triumph of caesar after mantegna and christ retiring from the judgment seat of pilate after relief by giambologna he was active in florence references andrea andreani in the encyclopædia britannica th edition vol ii getty ulan entry further reading artnet
