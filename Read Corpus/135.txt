styrkjärn in central norrköping the museum of work arbetetsmuseum is museum located in norrköping sweden the museum is located in the strykjärn former weaving mill in the old industrial area on the motala ström river in the city centre of norrköping the former textile factory holmens bruks och fabriks operated in the building from the museum documents work and everyday life by collecting personal stories about people professional lives from both the past and the present the museum archive contain material from memory collections and documentation projects since the museum also houses the ewk center for political illustration art which is based on work of the satirist ewert karlsson for decades he was frequently published in the swedish tabloid aftonbladet see also list of museums in sweden culture of sweden references external links arbetetsmuseum official site
