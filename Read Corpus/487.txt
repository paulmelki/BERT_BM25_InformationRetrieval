aimoin of fleury french chronicler was born at villefranche de longchat about and in early life entered the monastery of fleury where he became monk and passed the greater part of his life between and aimoin wrote about st benedict in abbey of fleury sur loire his chief work is historia francorum or libri de gestis francorum which deals with the history of the franks from the earliest times to and was continued by other writers until the middle of the twelfth century it was much in vogue during the middle ages but its historical value is now regarded as slight it was edited by waitz and published in the monumenta germaniae historica scriptores band xxvi hanover and berlin in he also wrote vita abbonis abbatis floriacensis the last of series of lives of the abbots of fleury all of which except the life of abbo have been lost this was published by mabillon in the acta sanctorum ordinis sancti benedicti paris aimoin third work was the composition of books ii and iii of the miracula sancti benedicti the first book of which was written by another monk of fleury named adrevald this also appears in the acta sanctorum references external links opera omnia by migne patrologia latina with analytical indexs
