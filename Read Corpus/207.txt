an elderly spinster she is one of christie best known characters and has been portrayed numerous times on screen her first appearance was in short story published in the royal magazine in december the tuesday night club which later became the first chapter of the thirteen problems her first appearance in full length novel was in the murder at the vicarage in and her last appearance was in sleeping
