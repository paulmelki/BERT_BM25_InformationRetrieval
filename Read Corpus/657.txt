coin of agathocles agathocles agathoklḗs bc was greek tyrant of syracuse bc and self styled king of sicily bc biography agathocles was born at thermae himeraeae modern name termini imerese in sicily the son of potter who had moved to syracuse in about bc he learned his father trade but afterwards entered the army along with his brother antander in bc he married the widow of his patron damas distinguished and wealthy citizen he was twice banished for attempting to overthrow the oligarchical party in syracuse in bc he returned with an army of mercenaries under solemn oath to observe the democratic constitution which was established after they took the city having massacred the oligarchs and the richest of the citizenry he thus made himself master of syracuse and he created strong army and fleet and subdued the greater part of sicily war with carthage followed in bc agathocles was defeated in the battle of the himera river and besieged in syracuse in bc he made desperate effort to break through the blockade and attack the enemy in africa in bc agathocles turned his attention towards ophellas ruler of cyrenaica as likely to prove useful ally in his war against the carthaginians in order to gain him over he promised to cede to him whatever conquests their combined forces might make in africa reserving to himself only the possession of sicily ophellas gathered powerful army from the homeland of his wife euthydike descendant of miltiades athens where many citizens felt disgruntled after having lost their voting rights notwithstanding all the natural obstacles which presented themselves on his route ophellas succeeded in reaching the carthaginian territories after toilsome and perilous march of more than two months duration he was received by agathocles with every demonstration of friendship and the two armies encamped near each other but not many days had elapsed when agathocles betrayed his new ally attacked the camp of the cyrenaeans and had ophellas himself killed the cyrenean troops left without leader went over to agathocles following several victories he was at last completely defeated bc and fled secretly to sicily after concluding peace with carthage in bc agathocles styled himself king of sicily in bc and established his rule over the greek cities of the island more firmly than ever peace treaty with carthage left him in control of sicily east of the halycus river even in his old age he displayed the same restless energy and is said to have been contemplating fresh attack on carthage at the time of his death his last years were plagued by ill health and the turbulence of his grandson archagathus at whose instigation he is said to have been poisoned according to others he died natural death he was born leader of mercenaries and although he did not shrink from cruelty to gain his ends he afterwards showed himself mild and popular tyrant agathocles restored the syracusan democracy on his death bed and did not want his sons to succeed him as king agathocles was married three times his first wife was the widow of his patron damas by whom he had two sons archagathus and his brother who were both
