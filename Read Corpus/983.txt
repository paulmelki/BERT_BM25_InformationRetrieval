action against hunger or action contre la faim acf in french is global humanitarian organization which originated in france and is committed to ending world hunger the organization helps malnourished children and provides communities with access to safe water and sustainable solutions to hunger in action against hunger worked in different countries around the world with more than employees and volunteers helping million people in need established in by group of french doctors scientists and writers nobel prize winning physicist alfred kastler served as the organization first chairman the group initially provided assistance to afghan refugees in pakistan famine stricken ugandan communities and cambodian refugees in thailand it expanded to address additional humanitarian concerns in africa the middle east southeast asia the balkans and elsewhere during the and action against hunger scientific committee pioneered the therapeutic milk formula now used by all major humanitarian aid organizations to treat acute malnutrition early results showed that treatment with has the capacity to reduce the mortality rate of severely malnourished children to below with median hospital fatality rate quoted of few years later therapeutic milk was repackaged as ready to use therapeutic foods rutfs peanut based paste packaged like power bar these bars allow for the treatment of
