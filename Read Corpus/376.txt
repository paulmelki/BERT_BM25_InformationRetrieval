the anadyr is river in the far northeast siberia which flows into the gulf of anadyr of the bering sea and drains much of the interior of chukotka autonomous okrug its basin corresponds to the anadyrsky district of chukotka geography the anadyr is long and has basin of it is frozen from october to late may and has maximum flow in june with the snowmelt it is navigable in small boats for
