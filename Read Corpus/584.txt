ambrogio traversari also referred to as ambrose of camaldoli october was an italian monk and theologian who was prime supporter of the papal cause in the th century he is honored as saint by the camaldolese order biography traversari was born near forlì in the village of portico di romagna in at the age of he entered the camaldolese order in the monastery of st mary of the angels in florence and rapidly became leading theologian and hellenist in his study of greek literature his master was emmanuel chrysoloras he worked primarily as scholar until he became prior general of the order in ambrose emerged as leading advocate of papal primacy this attitude he showed clearly when he attended the council of basel as legate of pope eugene iv and defended the primacy of the pope adjuring the council not to rend asunder christ seamless robe he was next sent by the pope to the emperor sigismund to ask his aid in the pope efforts to end this council which for five years had been encroaching on papal prerogatives the pope transferred the council from basel to ferrara on september so strong was ambrose hostility to some of the delegates that he described basel as western babylon he likewise supported the pope at ferrara and florence and worked hard in the attempt to reconcile the eastern and western churches but in this council and later in that of florence ambrose by his efforts and charity toward some poor greek bishops greatly helped to bring about union of the two churches the decree for which july he was called on to draw up he died soon after his feastday is
