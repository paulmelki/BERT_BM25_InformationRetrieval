agnes of merania died was queen of france she is called marie by some of the french chroniclers biography agnes maria was the daughter of berthold duke of merania who was count of andechs castle and territory near ammersee bavaria her mother was agnes of rochlitz in june agnes married philip ii of france who had repudiated his second wife ingeborg of denmark in pope innocent iii espoused the cause of ingeborg but philip did not submit until when nine months after interdict had been added to excommunication he consented to separation from agnes agnes died giving birth to their third child in july of the next year at the castle of poissy and was buried in the convent of st corentin near nantes family agnes and philip had two children philip count of boulogne and mary who were legitimized by the pope in at the request of the king little is known of the personality of agnes beyond the remarkable influence which she seems to have exercised over philip artistic representation she has been made the heroine of tragedy by françois ponsard agnès de méranie and of an opera by vincenzo bellini la straniera notes references endnotes see the notes of robert davidsohn in philipp ii august von frankreich und ingeborg stuttgart genealogical notice is furnished by the chronicon of the monk alberic aubry of fontaines albericus trium fontium in pertz scriptores vol xxiii pp and by the genealogia wettinensis ibid external links
