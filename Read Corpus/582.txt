autpert ambrose ambroise ca was frankish benedictine monk he became abbot of san vincenzo al volturno in south italy in the time of desiderius king of the lombards autpert election as abbot caused internal dissent at st vicenzo and both pope stephen iii and charlemagne intervened the disagreement was based both on objections to autpert personality and to his frankish origin he wrote considerable number of works on the bible and religious subjects generally among these are commentaries on the apocalypse on the psalms and on the song of solomon lives of saints paldo tuto and vaso assumption of the virgin and combat between the virtues and the vices in pope benedict xvi gave homily about him in saint peter square in this homily autpert death date is given as older scholarship had given date between and references external links
