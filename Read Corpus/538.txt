the triumph of time is poem by algernon charles swinburne published in poems and ballads in it is in adapted ottava rima and is full of elaborate use of literary devices particularly alliteration the theme which purports to be is that of rejected love the speaker deplores the ruin of his life and in tones at times reminiscent of hamlet craves oblivion for which the sea serves as constant metaphor notes see also poems and ballads external links complete text of the poem victorian web article on the poem
